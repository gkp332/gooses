Silly GOOSES and their own stories

1️⃣ The Bread Bandit "Gary the goose had one mission: steal bread. Every morning, he waited near the pond, eyeing picnickers. One day, he grabbed a whole baguette and ran—only to trip over his own feet. The ducks laughed. Gary, covered in crumbs, just honked in victory."

2️⃣ The Honk-Off Champion "Steve the goose challenged a human to a honk-off. The human tried their best, but Steve’s honks were so loud, car alarms went off. The human admitted defeat. Steve strutted away, knowing he was now a legend."

3️⃣ Goose Van Honkh "Leonardo the goose saw a human painting and got inspired. He grabbed a brush, dipped it in mud, and started ‘painting’ on a barn wall. The farmer called it a mess. The other geese called it ‘modern art.’ Leonardo honked proudly—true artists are never understood in their time."
